Privacy Policy for Guardrail

Effective date: March 24, 2026

Guardrail provides administrative and review functionality for Atlassian Jira.

1. Information Guardrail accesses

When installed in Jira, Guardrail may access information made available through Atlassian APIs and the permissions granted during installation, including:

  • Jira user information
  • Jira project and issue or work item information
  • Jira administrative configuration information
  • Settings and data created through Guardrail’s features

2. How Guardrail uses information

Guardrail uses this information to:

  • Generate and display access and review information
  • Support configuration of the app
  • Create remediation items in Jira when requested
  • Store app settings, audit records, snapshots, review records, and related operational data needed for app functionality

3. Storage

Guardrail stores app-related data using Atlassian Forge-hosted services and storage where applicable.

4. Sharing

Guardrail does not sell personal information.

Guardrail may share data only as necessary to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, enforce rights, or protect the security and integrity of the service.

5. Retention

Guardrail retains data only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the app, maintain records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

6. Security

Guardrail uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information processed by the app. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. Your choices

Because Guardrail operates within Atlassian products, many access, permission, and deletion choices are controlled by the Jira site administrator and Atlassian account settings.

8. Children

Guardrail is not directed to children.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be posted at this URL with the updated effective date.
